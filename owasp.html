About Us

Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology (TIET) has been a steady source of highly skilled talent for the nation and beyond. A pioneer in engineering education, research, and innovation. The OWASP Student Chapter is one of the premier coding societies at TIET, consisting of creative problem solvers eager to enhance security in the digital world. We organize hackathons, tech-talks, workshops,and coding nights to nurture the coding culture on campus.
